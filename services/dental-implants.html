Dental Implants

  1. home
  2. services

Dental implants are the gold standard for replacing missing teeth, providing a permanent, natural-looking solution that functions just like your original teeth. At Shri Sai Dental Clinic, we use advanced titanium implants that integrate with your jawbone to create a strong, stable foundation for crowns, bridges, or dentures. Our experienced team ensures precise placement and optimal healing for long-lasting results.

Unlike removable dentures or bridges that may require replacement over time, dental implants are designed to last a lifetime with proper care. They help preserve your facial structure, prevent bone loss, and restore full chewing function, allowing you to eat, speak, and smile with confidence.

Benefits of Dental Implants

Dental implants offer numerous advantages including improved oral health, enhanced appearance, better speech, increased comfort, and the convenience of permanent tooth replacement without affecting adjacent teeth.

  • permanent tooth replacement
  • natural look and feel
  • preserves jawbone health
  • high success rate
  • lifetime durability

Our implant procedures are performed with advanced surgical techniques and sedation options to ensure your comfort throughout the treatment process.

Common questions about dental implants

Most adults with good general health and adequate jawbone density are candidates for dental implants. We'll evaluate your oral health, medical history, and bone structure during consultation to determine if implants are right for you. Age is rarely a limiting factor.

The complete implant process typically takes 3-6 months. This includes the initial implant placement, healing period for osseointegration (bone fusion), and final crown placement. Some cases may be completed faster with immediate loading protocols.

Implant surgery is performed under local anesthesia, and sedation options are available for anxious patients. Most patients report minimal discomfort during and after the procedure. Any post-operative discomfort is typically manageable with over-the-counter pain medication.

Care for implants just like natural teeth with regular brushing, flossing, and dental checkups. Use a soft-bristled toothbrush and non-abrasive toothpaste. Regular professional cleanings and examinations are essential for long-term implant success.

Dental implants have a very high success rate of 95-98%. With proper care and regular dental visits, implants can last a lifetime. Factors like good oral hygiene, non-smoking, and regular checkups contribute to long-term success.
