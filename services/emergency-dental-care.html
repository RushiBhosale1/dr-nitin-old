Emergency Dental Care

Emergency dental care provides immediate treatment for urgent oral health issues that require prompt attention. It includes services such as severe toothaches, broken teeth, lost fillings, and dental trauma to prevent further complications. Emergency dentists address critical concerns from abscesses and bleeding gums to knocked-out teeth, ensuring rapid relief and preventing serious health risks. Emergency dental care is dedicated to delivering immediate care tailored to each patient's urgent needs, ensuring a comprehensive approach to dental emergencies that promotes quick recovery and prevents long-term damage.

Emergency services include immediate care such as pain relief, temporary repairs, and urgent treatments, which are essential for preventing infections, severe complications, and permanent damage. Emergency dentists perform urgent procedures like temporary fillings, tooth reattachment, and abscess drainage to address critical issues, as well as provide pain management and antibiotics when necessary.

Everything you need to know about emergency dental care

Dental emergencies include severe toothaches, broken or knocked-out teeth, lost fillings, abscesses, bleeding gums, and dental trauma. If you're experiencing intense pain or have suffered dental injury, contact us immediately for urgent care.

We prioritize emergency cases and typically see urgent patients within 1-2 hours of contact. For severe emergencies, we can often accommodate same-day appointments to provide immediate relief and treatment.

For a knocked-out tooth, gently rinse it with water, avoid touching the root, and try to reinsert it into the socket. If that's not possible, place it in milk or saliva and contact us immediately. Time is crucial for successful reattachment.

Yes, we prioritize immediate pain relief for emergency patients. We can provide local anesthesia, prescribe pain medications, and perform emergency procedures to alleviate severe dental pain as quickly as possible.

For dental emergencies, call us immediately at +91 92268 07779. We provide 24/7 emergency support and can arrange urgent appointments for severe cases requiring immediate attention.
